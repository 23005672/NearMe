OOTY TREE PARK

NATURE'S PARADISE IN THE NILGIRIS

Ooty Tree Park is a serene and beautifully maintained eco-park located in the scenic town of Ooty, Tamil Nadu. Nestled in the heart of the Nilgiri Hills, it showcases a wide variety of native and exotic trees. The park is a perfect spot for nature lovers, offering peaceful walking paths and scenic views.

Park Highlights

Diverse Flora: Features a rich collection of tree species native to the Nilgiris and other regions.

Walking Trails: Well-paved paths for leisurely walks amidst lush greenery.

Educational Value: Informational boards about each tree make it an ideal spot for learning.

Accessibility: Easily accessible by road and well-connected to other tourist attractions in Ooty.
