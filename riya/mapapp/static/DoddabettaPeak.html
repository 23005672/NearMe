DODDABETTA PEAK

THE HIGHEST POINT IN THE NILGIRIS

Doddabetta Peak is the highest mountain in the Nilgiri Hills, located near Ooty, Tamil Nadu. Standing at an elevation of 2,637 meters, it offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys. The peak is a popular tourist spot, known for its scenic beauty and cool climate.

Peak Features

Viewpoint: Equipped with a telescope house for tourists to enjoy distant views of the landscape.

Flora & Fauna: Surrounded by dense Shola forests, home to a variety of plant and bird species.

Accessibility: Located about 9 km from Ooty, it is easily reachable by road.

Photography Spot: A favorite among nature photographers and hikers for capturing misty mountain scenes.
